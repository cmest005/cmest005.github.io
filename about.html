About me

My name is Carlos Andres Mestre. I am a 1st generation Cuban-American from Miami, Florida. I have a strong core of family and friends that I find very valuable. Recently, in December 2016, I graduated from Florida International University with a B.A. in Computer Science. Working with computers has always been a strong passion of mine. Like many 90s kids I started off with video games, and developed that love of technology into putting together my own PC. On my resume, you may see that I also have a B.A. in Business Administration. These two degrees give me a strong foundations for doing what I love; working in a fast-paced environment that promotes teamwork.


Hobbies
