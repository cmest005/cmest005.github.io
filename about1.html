About me

My name is Carlos Andres Mestre. I am a 1st generation Cuban-American from Miami, Florida. I have a strong core of family and friends that are very important to me. Recently, in December 2016, I graduated from Florida International University with a B.A. in Computer Science. Working with computers has always been a strong passion of mine. Prior to earning my degree in Computer Science, I graduated from University of North Florida with a Bachelors in Business Administration. These two degrees give me a strong foundations for doing what I love, working as a programmer in a fast-paced environment that promotes teamwork.
